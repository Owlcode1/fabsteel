Frequently Asked Questions

What is the steel deatailing ?

Steel detailing is a specialized process used in construction to produce detailed drawings and 3D models that guide the fabrication and erection of structural steel components. It ensures every beam, column, connection, and weld is accurately represented, helping fabricators and builders assemble the structure efficiently and without errors.

What software does Fab Steel use for steel detailing ?

We primarily use Tekla Structures, a powerful 3D modeling tool, to create accurate and detailed steel models. It allows us to generate reliable fabrication and erection drawings for all types of structural steel.

How does Fab Steel ensure quality and accuracy ?

Our team conducts thorough reviews and testing at each stage of the detailing process. Using advanced tools and industry standards, we ensure every drawing and model is accurate, buildable, and error-free.

Can Fab Steel handle international projects ?

Yes! Fab Steel Engineering Services works with clients worldwide, delivering detailed, high-quality steel detailing solutions regardless of location or project size.
